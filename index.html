      ✦ TECHNOLOGY

      How ChefAI Works

      ChefAI uses advanced language AI combined with nutrition science to craft recipes tailored to you.

      🧠

      Large Language Model (LLM)

      ChefAI uses Claude, an advanced large language model trained on millions of recipes, culinary techniques, and food science data. It understands ingredient pairings, cooking chemistry, and cultural context to produce coherent, delicious recipes from scratch.

      🎛️

      Preference-Conditioned Prompting

      Your inputs (ingredients, diet, allergies, cuisine, skill level) are converted into a structured prompt. The AI uses these as constraints to generate recipes that strictly respect your lifestyle — ensuring vegan, gluten-free, or keto compliance automatically.

      🥗

      Nutritional Optimization

      Beyond taste, ChefAI considers your nutritional goals. Whether you're building muscle, losing weight, or maintaining energy, the AI adjusts portion sizes, ingredient ratios, and cooking methods to align with your macro and micronutrient needs.

      🔄

      Iterative Refinement

      Each recipe generation is unique. The AI doesn't just template-fill — it reasons through ingredient compatibility, identifies flavor profiles, balances textures, and applies cooking principles to create something genuinely novel every time you click generate.

      🛡️

      Allergy-Safe Filtering

      Allergy flags are treated as hard constraints in the AI's generation pipeline. The model is instructed to avoid all derivatives and hidden forms of allergens (e.g., wheat starch, casein from dairy), keeping your recipe not just personalized but safe.

      🌍

      Cultural & Culinary Intelligence

      ChefAI has deep knowledge of global cuisine traditions. When you select Italian or Thai, it adapts spice profiles, cooking techniques, and presentation styles authentically — not just surface-level labels, but genuine culinary character.
